ABOUT THE CON

Three days of pure comic book madness!

Join thousands of fans, creators, and industry professionals for the biggest comic convention in Metro City! Discover new comics, meet your favorite artists, attend exclusive panels, and immerse yourself in the world of superheroes, manga, indie comics, and pop culture. Whether you're a longtime collector or just getting started, Metro City Comic Con has something amazing for everyone!
